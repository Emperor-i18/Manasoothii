Practices to Maintain Good Mental Health

A good mental health state allows you to manage stress and enjoy life. Consider integrating these habits:

  • Practice mindfulness or deep breathing daily.
  • Stay physically active with regular exercise.
  • Maintain social connections and strong relationships.
  • Prioritize restful sleep and balanced nutrition.
  • Set realistic goals and allow time for self-care.
  • Limit exposure to overwhelming or negative information.
