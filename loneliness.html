What is Loneliness?

Loneliness is a complex emotional state where a person feels disconnected and isolated, even when around others.

It can impact both mental and physical health, possibly increasing risks of depression, anxiety, and other health issues.

Ways to Prevent and Manage Loneliness

  • Engage in hobbies and activities that bring you joy.
  • Maintain regular contact with friends and family via calls or visits.
  • Join community groups, clubs, or social events to foster connections.
  • Practice self-compassion and mindfulness to nurture your well-being.
