What is Depression?

Depression is a common mood disorder characterized by persistent sadness, loss of interest, and impaired daily functioning.

Effective management and early intervention can improve quality of life and help in recovery.

Ways to Manage Depression

  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule and balanced nutrition.
  • Engage in physical activity and outdoor time daily.
  • Practice mindfulness meditation and relaxation exercises.
  • Seek professional support such as counseling or therapy when needed.

Breathing Exercise for Depression Relief

Try this deep breathing technique to help manage depressive symptoms:

  1. Sit comfortably with a straight back and relaxed shoulders.
  2. Inhale slowly and deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
  3. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.
  5. Repeat this cycle for 5-10 minutes to promote relaxation.
